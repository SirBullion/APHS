Our Services

At All People Holistic Support, we deliver culturally informed, person-centred services to support our communities across the Northern Territory.

NDIS Services

  • Core Supports (e.g. personal care, transport, daily living assistance)
  • Community Access & Participation
  • Supported Independent Living (SIL)
  • Short-Term Accommodation (STA)

Youth Engagement

  • Mentoring and Life Skills
  • Cultural Programs and Safe Spaces
  • Early Intervention & Capacity Building

Liaison & Community Outreach

  • Cultural Brokerage and Advocacy
  • Support in Remote and Regional Communities
  • Connection to Country and Kin

Who We Support

At All People Holistic Support, we are proud to support individuals, families, and communities across all regions of the Northern Territory. Our services are designed to be inclusive, culturally respectful, and tailored to the needs of those we serve.

NDIS Participants

We provide personalised support to NDIS participants, helping them build capacity, achieve their goals, and live more independently with services that are flexible and participant-focused.

Young People

Through one-on-one mentoring and youth engagement programs, we support young people in developing life skills, building confidence, and creating positive pathways for their future.

Families

We offer outreach and practical support for families, with a focus on culturally appropriate care. Our team works collaboratively with families to strengthen wellbeing, preserve cultural identity, and promote positive outcomes for children and caregivers.

Community Liaison

Our liaison services play a vital role in bridging the gap between service providers and community members. We facilitate effective communication, cultural understanding, and access to resources by working closely with Elders, community leaders, and local organisations.
