About Us

All People Holistic Support We are an Aboriginal-owned and community-led provider of culturally informed NDIS, youth, and liaison services. We proudly serve Darwin, Alice Springs, Arnhem Land, and remote communities across the Northern Territory — delivering real care through connection, community, and culture. Our approach is grounded in deep respect for Country, culture, and the lived experiences of the people we work with. By centring Aboriginal voices and leadership, we create stronger, more responsive services that truly meet the needs of our communities.

Why Choose Us

  • Culturally Grounded & Informed: We are deeply rooted in Indigenous ways of knowing, doing, and being. Our services are built with cultural safety at the center, guided by lived experience and connection to Country.
  • Holistic Support That Sees the Whole Person: We go beyond just ticking boxes — we walk alongside our clients with care that supports their spirit, body, mind, and community. Our approach listens first and empowers always.
  • Aboriginal Owned & Community-Driven: We are led by Mob, for Mob. As an Aboriginal-owned provider, we know the real needs of our communities — and we work to bridge the gap between expectation and reality with dignity, trust, and heart.

Our Vision

To empower our people through culturally responsive services that bridge the gap between systemic support and community needs.

Our Values

  • Cultural Safety: Prioritising identity, language, and traditional ways of knowing.
  • Empowerment: Building independence through strengths-based supports.
  • Respect: Valuing every individual’s journey, from elders to youth.
  • Connection: Grounding care in kinship, land, and community.

Who We Support

Our services support NDIS participants aged 25–68, young people needing mentorship, and families requiring culturally appropriate care and outreach in remote NT communities.
